# Adidas Sales Analysis (BI)

## Overview
This project analyzes Adidas sales performance to identify trends across time, products, regions, and retailers. The deliverable is a presentation deck summarizing insights and recommendations to support business decision-making.

## Business Questions
- How do sales and operating profit trend over time across product categories?
- Which regions and retailers contribute most to total sales and operating profit?
- How do operating margin and profitability vary by category and region?
- What patterns indicate growth, maturity, or decline across categories?

## Deliverables
- Presentation (PDF): [docs/Adidas Sales Analysis PPT Deck.pdf](docs/Adidas%20Sales%20Analysis%20PPT%20Deck.pdf)
- Presentation (PPTX): [docs/Adidas Sales Analysis PPT Deck.pptx](docs/Adidas%20Sales%20Analysis%20PPT%20Deck.pptx)

## Key Insights
- Centralized view of revenue, profit, margin, and units sold across product and region segments.
- Identified top contributing regions and retailers, plus segments with optimization opportunities.
- Highlighted category-level margin differences to support pricing and inventory decisions.

## Approach
- Reviewed and validated dataset fields, consistency, and reporting KPIs.
- Defined KPIs: Total Sales, Operating Profit, Operating Margin, Units Sold, Price per Unit.
- Performed trend, regional, retailer, and category performance analysis.
- Built visuals and structured the narrative into a client-ready deck.
